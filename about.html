OUR STORY

Building Confident Drivers Since Day One

SP Driving Training School was founded with a single mission — to help every person in Pune develop genuine road confidence, not just pass a test. Over 12+ years, we have grown into one of the most trusted and highly-rated driving schools in the Dhanori and Vishrantwadi area.

What started as a small school with a vision for quality has evolved into a certified institution holding some of India's most prestigious driving-education credentials — including the rare All Gujarat Institute of Driving, Technical Training & Research certification, managed by Maruti Suzuki.

We believe that real driving skill comes from time on the road, unlimited practice, and patient instruction — not from counting kilometres or rushing through sessions. That's why every 1-hour session at SP comes with unlimited KM.

HOW WE TEACH

The 8 / H / Appeal Test Method

We specifically prepare every student using the official RTO driving test pattern. Our method ensures you pass first time.

8

Figure-8 Pattern

Master the figure-eight driving pattern used in the official RTO test — essential for showing vehicle control and precision steering.

H

H-Pattern

Navigate the H-shaped track used in RTO practical tests, testing your ability to drive forward, reverse, and manoeuvre in tight spaces.

A

Appeal Pattern

The Appeal driving test pattern assesses road sense, signal compliance, and smooth vehicle control under examiner observation.

+

Theory + Practical

We complement all on-road training with intellectual theory classes covering road signs, traffic laws, and safe driving principles.

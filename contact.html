FAQ

Quick Answers

How long is each training session?+
Each session is 1 hour long. Unlike other schools, we offer unlimited KM in that 1 hour — for both 2-wheeler and 4-wheeler. No KM cap.
What is the Ghat road training?+
We are specialists in Ghat road driving — steep inclines, hairpin bends, downhill engine braking. Very few schools in Pune offer this. It's available for both 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers.
Do you help with the RTO licence?+
Yes, completely. We handle all RTO documentation, guide you through the learner's licence process, and prepare you for the road test using the 8/H/Appeal method so you pass first time.
Are night sessions available?+
Yes! We offer special night driving sessions for professionals and students with busy daytime schedules. WhatsApp us to book a night session slot.
Do you have weekend batches?+
Absolutely. Saturday and Sunday batches are available. Please book in advance as weekend slots fill quickly.
What is the All Gujarat Institute certification?+
It's a prestigious driving-education certification managed by Maruti Suzuki. Very few schools in Maharashtra hold it. It certifies our instructor quality, training methods, and student outcomes to the highest national standards.
What are your fees?+
Fees vary by course (Learner, Advanced, Extended, 2-Wheeler, etc.). WhatsApp or call us for transparent, up-to-date pricing — no hidden charges.

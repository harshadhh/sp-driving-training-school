⚠️ Important — Read This First
You cannot upload a video file directly — browsers only allow 5MB storage. Videos are much bigger.

▶ YOUTUBE
Option 1 — YouTube Link (Easiest)
Upload your video to YouTube → paste the link here. Video opens on YouTube when clicked. Takes 2 minutes.
1. Go to youtube.com → click your profile → Upload video
2. Pick your video → set visibility to Public or Unlisted
3. After upload → copy the page URL (youtube.com/watch?v=…) → paste below
☁ CLOUDINARY
Option 2 — Cloudinary Link (Plays on your site)
Upload to Cloudinary → video plays directly on your website with full controls. Best experience.
1. Go to cloudinary.com → sign up free
2. Click Media Library → Upload → pick your video
3. After upload → click the video → click Copy URL → it ends in .mp4
4. Paste that .mp4 URL below ✅
